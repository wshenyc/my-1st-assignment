We're in a Housing Crisis

The economic crisis caused by COVID-19 has put nearly 40 million people at risk of eviction. In an attempt to keep tenants housed, New York implemented a Rental Relief Program. However, the state only managed to distribute $40M of the $100M available in federal aid. Not for lack of interest — 94,000 applications were received — but 60% were denied due to the stringent eligibility requirements.
